Help Getting started with HTM

Welcome to HTM (Home Thermal Management). In the picture above it is the way you are presented the first look of the application.
There are 3 categories: Unassigned Devices, Rooms, Outside Sensors
Unassigned Devices will be populated when devices are connected to the same router with the HUB
In Rooms you will control your house devices
Outside devices will provide information to the AI (Artificial Inteligence) regarding the outside enviroment.


By clicking the "Add room" button you can add new rooms. After clicking apply an alert will pop.


This is how the application will look after assigning a few devices.


Here are the devices that are not used for anything. With Drag&Drop feature you can assign them to a specific room or for outside.
NOTE: Only 'sensors' can be assigned to outside area.


It's just that simple! you Drag&Drop and it is paired!


Here multiple rooms can be selected for removing
NOTE: Assigned devices to that room are sent to 'Unassigned Devices' if the room is removed.


You can change your mind and change the settings. After selecting a room and pressing the 'Cange preferences' button you are met with a new card.


In this card after recompleting the desired preferences and clicking the 'Apply' button your settings are changed.


By double clicking a room you can quickly see what are the current settings for that room.


Sensors have the antenna picture. This picture refers to a device, example (heater,air conditioner,air purifier, etc.).
Devices paired to a specific room will inform the AI what it can do to keep your enviroment delightful.
